About Symptom Prediction

Our advanced machine learning model uses Gated Recurrent Units (GRU) to analyze your symptom history and identify patterns over time. The model looks at:

  • Symptom frequency and severity
  • Time-based patterns (day of week, time of day)
  • Correlations between different symptoms
  • Progression patterns over time

The more symptom data you log, the more accurate our predictions will become. These predictions are meant to help you anticipate potential health issues and take preventive measures.

Note: This is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider for serious health concerns.
