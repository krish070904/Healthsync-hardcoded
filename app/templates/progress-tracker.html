Health Tracking Tips
  • Measure your weight at the same time each day, preferably in the morning after using the bathroom and before eating.
  • For blood pressure, sit quietly for 5 minutes before measuring and avoid caffeine or exercise for 30 minutes prior.
  • Log your measurements consistently to get the most accurate trends and insights.
  • Set realistic health goals based on your doctor's recommendations.
