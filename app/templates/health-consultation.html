About Health Consultation

The HealthSync Health Consultation feature provides personalized health insights based on your tracked data:

  • General Health Assessment: Analysis of your overall health status based on your tracked metrics.
  • Vital Signs Assessment: Evaluation of your blood pressure, blood sugar, and other vital signs.
  • Symptom Analysis: Patterns and trends in your reported symptoms.
  • Nutrition Assessment: Analysis of your dietary habits and nutritional intake.
  • Food-Symptom Correlations: Identification of potential food triggers for your symptoms.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored advice to improve your health based on your data.

Note: This consultation is based on rule-based analysis of your tracked data and should not replace professional medical advice.
